Official Resolution of The Director

**RESOLUTION OF THE DIRECTOR OF TRIPOT (PTY) LTD**
(Registration Number: 2021/132487/07)

**PASSED AT A MEETING HELD ON THIS 26TH DAY OF JUNE 2025 AT [CURRENT TIME]**

**WHEREAS:**

1. TRIPOT (PTY) LTD (hereinafter referred to as "the Company") is a company duly incorporated in accordance with the company laws of the Republic of South Africa, with registration number 2021/132487/07.

2. Mr. Heyns Schoeman, Identity Number 8603035153082, is the sole director of the Company.

3. The Company conducts a wholesale business and is currently experiencing financial distress due to unforeseen delays in stock imports, which has led to unpaid creditors and an inability to meet current financial obligations, including rental arrears for its premises at 40 Zanie Straat, Newmark Estate, Pretoria.

4. A court date is set for 1 July 2025 for the landlord to execute a hypothec over the Company's movable assets, including essential office equipment, situated at the aforementioned premises.

5. Despite the current challenges, the Company possesses valuable inventory that will be captured and valued, has certainty regarding existing sales orders, and maintains strong relationships with importers who are holding significant private label stock for the Company and are willing to supply on a Cash on Delivery (COD) basis, enabling managed sales to national retail chains.

6. The director believes that there are reasonable prospects for rescuing the Company and restoring it to solvency, thereby preserving employment, maximizing the likelihood of the Company continuing in existence on a solvent basis, and yielding a better return for creditors than would result from immediate liquidation.

7. The initiation of business rescue proceedings is necessary to prevent the immediate sale of essential assets, facilitate the structured management of current inventory and incoming stock, and allow the Company to leverage its established vendor relationships and sales orders to generate revenue and repay its obligations.

8. A key component of the business rescue plan involves the potential reinstatement of staff with good qualities and long-term relationships to effectively drive sales and accelerate the Company's recovery.

9. The director, being the sole director of the Company, hereby deems it necessary and appropriate, in terms of Section 129(1) of the Companies Act, 71 of 2008, to place the Company under business rescue.

**IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED THAT:**

1. The Company, TRIPOT (PTY) LTD, hereby voluntarily commences business rescue proceedings, effective immediately, in terms of Section 129(1) of the Companies Act, 71 of 2008.

2. Mr. Heyns Schoeman, in his capacity as the sole director, is hereby authorized to take all necessary steps to formally file the Notice of Beginning of Business Rescue Proceedings (Form CoR123.1) with the Companies and Intellectual Property Commission (CIPC) without delay.

3. The director is further authorized to engage with the Company’s legal representatives to appoint a suitably qualified, experienced, and cost-efficient business rescue practitioner as soon as practically possible, as required by the Companies Act.

4. All creditors of the Company shall be notified of these proceedings within the prescribed timelines as stipulated by the Companies Act.

5. The director is authorized to do all such things and sign all such documents as may be necessary or incidental to give effect to this resolution, including but not limited to, actions relating to the strategic reinstatement of key personnel to support business continuity and sales objectives.
